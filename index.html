Who We Are

We are a group of design driven individuals passionate about creating beautiful UI and professionally engineered products.

Elight has a select class of engineers and UI designers who have come together to help founders convert there imagination to products that cusotmers and investors admire. Why do you need us? Once founders have a brilliant idea that they believe has the potential! The next step becomes to convey their idea into a prototype and then into a working prototype that they can show to customers and investors. Its important to convey that imagination in the right way and in a way such the everybody understands. Hiring full-time engineers who you have to feed a lot is hard at this stage and it doesnt make sense as everyday such engineers wont have work as you are still trying to figure out the market and decide on the roadmap and features that should actually go into the product. We work directly with founders to convert and capture all the intricacies of the imagination and help founders reach the product-market fit.! When they are capable enough to hire full-time engineers themselves and scale more and more!

Define

We help founders define the milestones from the engineering and design standpoints. And make it clear on what is going to happen at the end of the contract when the product will be deliveverd and what to expect as the founder interacts with us for sequential feature embedding.

Design

Elight consists of some of the best deisgners. Our designers and founders sit on a call to discuss the design and flow of the product and if the outcome is something that looks good to the founders. The rough design/full-fledged design can be provided by the founder incase they are willing. Else the Elight Team will help build the design and flow.

Build

Best in class Engineers to build Web, Android, iOS applications for any need. The Elight team will help you build the Minimum Lovable Product that your customer adores and investors see a potential in. The Engineering stack can be chosen as per by founders if any preference else we go with the stack that best suites the product and has the potential to easily scale when customers start coming in. We also have onboarded Cybersecurity professionals who assist in the entire process to make sure the application is secure.

Launch

The Elight Team will host the application in the discussed cloud, be it AWS, GCP or Azure. We will create the entire initial infrastruture for you and you dont need to even move a muscle. Just relax and imagine. We also help in building CI/CD pipelines for one shot automatic deployment. And also in populating your initial database and keeping the scripts right in place. So whenever required you can start a new instance or a local deployment of your database.
